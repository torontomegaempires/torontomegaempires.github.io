Winter Events

Thanks everyone who came out for Mega Empires: The East Game 16!
We had three new players join us which is fantastic to see the player pool growing. Congratulations to Sam who won the game playing as Saba! If you didn't manage to get into the game, read on because there is a lot more happening this winter with Mega Empires in Toronto.

Mega Empires @ The Guild House
Saturday January 10

Our 2nd annual game of Mega Empires will be at the Guild House again!
We had 15 players last year, let's see if we can get to a full 18 this time around!

Mega Empires: The West
Niagara Board Gaming Weekend
Thursday January 15

If you're planning to attend the Niagara Board Gaming Weekend, I'll be running a Mega Empires: The West game to kick things off on Thursday.

Mega Empires: The East - Game 17
Saturday February 28

Our normal 9 player home game will feature the East again before we switch back to the West in May.

Mega Empires @ Breakout Con
Friday March 20

Now that Breakout Con is 4 days I am going to be running a game on the Friday. Come play with brand new people!
