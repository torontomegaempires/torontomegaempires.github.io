How It Played Out

Our inaugural game hosted by the UoT Table Top Gaming Club was an outstanding success! The venue was great — a huge table, great lighting, and a quiet environment for us to yell trade deals at each other.

A few notable moments:

  • Rome (Wai W.) was a calamity magnet, getting hit with 6 or more calamities not once but twice in the game. The 1-2 punch of Civil War and Tyranny ended the Roman Empire and brought Iberia to the fore.
  • The game featured a lot of empires sustaining 8 and 9 cities for multiple turns — an unusually city-heavy affair.
